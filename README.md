# Cloth

A 22×22 particle mesh held by structural and shear springs, each node softly anchored to its rest position so the surface deforms under pressure and ripples back. Move the cursor across the mesh to push nodes out of place — connections under strain glow brighter as tension builds. Click to send a shockwave through the entire grid at once.

**Interaction:** Move cursor to poke and deform the mesh · Click to fire a shockwave  
**Tech:** Canvas 2D, structural and shear spring simulation, tension-based rendering
